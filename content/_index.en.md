## About WIDE Project Tsukuba NOC

WIDE Project Tsukuba NOC conducts academic network operations and research activities at the University of Tsukuba. We provide various services with the goal of contributing to the development and wider adoption of Internet technologies.

## About WIDE Project

WIDE Internet is a testbed network operated by the WIDE Project. Since the project was launched in 1988, it has provided Internet access to a wide range of universities, companies, and research institutions.
