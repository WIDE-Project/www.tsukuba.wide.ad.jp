This page lists the activities and members of Tsukuba NOC.

## Current activities

- **Participation in the WIDE Project as a whole**: Participation in WIDE Camp and cooperation in maintaining the WIDE Project backbone.
- **Network operations technology research**: We conduct technical research related to network operations, including monitoring, fault detection, and automation.
- **Public FTP server operation**: We operate mirror servers for various Linux distributions and other software, providing a high-speed download environment.
- **IPv6 tunnel service (WIP)**: We provide an IP6IP tunnel service that supports IPv6 connectivity experiments over IPv4 networks.
